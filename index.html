Power BI

Maven Analytics Challenge — Return to Space

An analytical look at the history of shuttle launches, the golden age of space flight, and the organizations leading modern missions.

Key Insights

  • USA led with 1,468 successful missions, narrowly ahead of Russia (1,416).
  • RVSN USSR was the most successful organization with 1,777 launches.
  • The 1970s marked the golden era with 1,012 missions.
  • Overall mission success rate stabilized at 92.7% since the 1980s.
  • Most reliable vehicle: Cosmos-3 (11K65M).
  • Lowest cost per mission: $2.5M (Rocket 3).
